# M10 next workload plan — schedule lineage and supersession archival

- superseded receipts still accumulate as live surface noise,
- schedule-cycle history is not yet compacted into one explicit lineage view,
- and the system still lacks one bounded archival witness for prior schedule lines.

## Goal

Land one bounded M10 surface for:
- explicit schedule-line lineage summary,
- supersession archival / compaction witness,

- hidden compaction of authoritative records
- free-form schedule rewriting outside existing scheduler law
