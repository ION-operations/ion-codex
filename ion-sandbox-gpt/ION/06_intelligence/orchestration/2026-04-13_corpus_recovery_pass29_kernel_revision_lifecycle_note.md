## Purpose

Pass 29 strengthens the frozen-kernel change layer by adding:
- an approval matrix,
- a revision lifecycle,

## Why this matters

The project already had a thaw protocol, but it still needed a clearer path for deciding:
- what depth of evidence a kernel change requires,
- how a change moves from trigger to re-freeze,
- and how to keep revision proposals from becoming oversized or vague.
