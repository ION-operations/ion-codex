# Corpus Recovery Pass 16 — Packet-Gate Control Layer

## Purpose

Pass 16 turns the atlas operating layer into a stricter future-work control surface.

## What changed

Added:
- `future_work_packet_requirements.md`
- `bounded_extension_vs_illegal_reinvention.md`
- `atlas_gated_work_start_record_template.md`
- `atlas_packet_gate_checklist.md`

## Why it matters

Earlier passes made the atlas the best place to start. Pass 16 makes it harder for future work to claim it started from the atlas without recording how it actually consulted the recovered centers and conflicts first.

## Result

The atlas operating layer now includes:
- authority selection
- conflict consultation
- work posture selection
- anti-reinvention judgment
- and a minimum work-start record

This is intended to reduce one of the main recurring project failures: plausible local work that begins from the wrong surviving center and silently reinvents something that already exists elsewhere in the estate.
