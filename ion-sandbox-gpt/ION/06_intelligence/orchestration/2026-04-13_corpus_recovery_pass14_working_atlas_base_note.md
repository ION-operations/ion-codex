# Pass 14 — working atlas base note

## Purpose

Pass 14 does not widen the recovery tree. It stabilizes it into a more usable **working atlas base**.

## What was added

- a Milestone 1 freeze manifest
- a working atlas base entry surface
- a working center-selection guide

## Why it matters

The recovery program now tells future work not just what has been recovered, but **which preserved center to consult first** for each major kind of question. This is meant to reduce false replacement and reduce the chance that future work re-loses the organism by starting from the wrong center.
