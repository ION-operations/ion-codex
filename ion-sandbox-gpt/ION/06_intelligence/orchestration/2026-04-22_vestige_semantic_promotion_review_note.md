Yes.

- stale-surface detection
- lineage drift watch
- open-thread preservation
- evidence-bound surfacing of buried contradictions

That is already a real and durable role burden.

### 4. Does Vestige have stable template expectations or stable packet families?

Yes, at a minimally adequate level.

But it does already have stable and repeated output families backed by protocol and contract law:

- `alerts/`

That is enough to support promotion review honestly.
It also means a follow-on archaeology binding or packet-family hardening pass is still advisable.

### 5. Would Vestige still be the same role if the chassis changed?

Yes.

The active branch commonly mounts Vestige on `Composer 2`, but the role law is not about one model.
It is about persistent excavation, read breadth, bounded write scope, and evidence surfacing across the active field.

### 6. Is Vestige now producing enough semantic drift risk that boot-plus-mount is no longer sufficient?

Yes.

Vestige lives close to some of the branch’s most drift-sensitive burdens:

- stale authority
- buried contradictions
- provenance gaps
- open unresolved threads

If that role stays indefinitely boot-plus-mount only, there is an avoidable risk that archaeology collapses back into generic research, informal scavenging, or ad hoc support labor.

The protocol and lane already show that this burden is more specific than that.

## Current judgment

`Vestige` now satisfies the current-phase semantic-promotion criteria strongly enough that promotion is supportable and recommended.

The honest sequencing judgment is still:

1. `Relay` first
2. `Vestige` second

That is not because Vestige is weak.
It is because Relay resolves the more immediate front-door ambiguity, while Vestige resolves the more internal archaeology/continuity ambiguity.

## What promotion should and should not mean

Promotion should mean:

- stabilize archaeology as a real persistent role
- preserve the distinction between archaeology, research, contradiction pressure, and audit
- protect the role from collapsing back into generic research labor

Promotion should **not** mean:

- give Vestige release authority
- give Vestige architecture authority
- give Vestige audit verdict authority
- convert Vestige into a free-dispatch intelligence manager

The role should remain broad-read, narrow-write, and evidence-bound.
