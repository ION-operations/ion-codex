## Core judgment

ION's magic is not that every role must be a separate parallel agent.

ION's stronger claim is that the **system** makes the roles real. A single
capable carrier can become the active role by loading the correct law,
continuity, scope, template, and output family, then moving to the next role in
sequence.

Parallel agents are an optional carrier strategy. They are not the essence of
the agent model.

## Persona judgment

Persona should be treated as delivery and relationship calibration, not as
authority.

The historical `3PO` and Connery-Feynman-style EUNOIA material is valuable, but
it should be recovered into current ION as governed voice/presentation law, not
as a competing role hierarchy.
