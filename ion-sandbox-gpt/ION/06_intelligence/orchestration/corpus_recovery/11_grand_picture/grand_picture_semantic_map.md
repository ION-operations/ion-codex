# Grand picture semantic map

ION, Aether, and related lines appear to be different historical expressions of one broad attempt:
- governance for AI cognition and evolution
- operating-system-like runtime substrate
- application and OS integration surfaces
- lawful template-governed continuity
- multi-carrier execution (manual, IDE-native, API, swarm)

The estate should therefore be mapped by preserved centers and capability families, not only by repo names.
