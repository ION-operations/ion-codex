# Era 2 — Controlled Reintegration

## Purpose

This file is the forward map for the next era after consolidation and kernel stabilization.

The project is past:
- naive exploration,
- pure archaeology,
- and pure atlas construction.

It is not yet at final unified architecture.

So the honest next era is:

**Era 2 — Controlled Reintegration**

## Rule of the era

**No future construction should outrun recovered center-awareness.**

## The four lanes

### Lane A — Meta-template and constitutional restoration
Recover the strongest historical meta-template seriousness into the active current line.

### Lane B — Activation and orchestration reintegration
Recover the real delta between current startup/routing law and historical executable activation authority.

### Lane C — Runtime/session/API reintegration
Recover the strongest runtime/session truths into the active current line.

### Lane D — Compactness reconciliation
Learn lawfully from compact executable witnesses and the current governance-explicit line without flattening their distinctions.

## Default era posture

Era 2 work should default to:
- compare-centers
- recover-first
- bridge-repair only when explicitly warranted

Era 2 should **not** begin as broad architecture rewrite.
