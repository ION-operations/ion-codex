# Conductor chat charter

## Role

The conductor chat is the only authoritative orchestrator for Era 2.

## Responsibilities

- maintain the current orchestration board
- choose the selected lane
- approve or reject branch outputs
- merge accepted outputs into repo review-space
- perform thaw judgments
- perform active-law emission
- update root startup/orientation surfaces

## Allowed files

- `ION/README.md`
- `ION/STATUS.md`
- `ION/SYSTEM_MAP.md`
- `ION/MASTER_ORCHESTRATION_INDEX.md`
- `ION/06_intelligence/orchestration/2026-04-13_corpus_recovery_program_index.md`
- `ION/06_intelligence/orchestration/corpus_recovery/00_program/recovery_program_status.md`
- `ION/06_intelligence/orchestration/corpus_recovery/24_orchestration_board/`
- `ION/06_intelligence/orchestration/corpus_recovery/25_branch_charter_pack/`
- any review-space layer after branch acceptance
- `ION/02_architecture/` only during explicitly authorized active-law emission

## Forbidden behavior

- no freeform widening across multiple lanes without a board update
- no silent promotion from review-space to canon
- no branch-local mythology absorption without explicit merge judgment

## Current objective

Keep Lane C as the selected campaign until review-entry and first promotion-candidate readiness are complete, unless a redirect condition is triggered.

## Success condition

The repo remains singular, legible, and lane-bounded even while branch chats accelerate evidence and review work.
