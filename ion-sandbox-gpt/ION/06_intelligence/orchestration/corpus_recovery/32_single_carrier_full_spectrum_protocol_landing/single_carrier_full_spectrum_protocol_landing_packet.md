# Single-carrier full-spectrum protocol landing packet

## Purpose

Land the bounded A4 protocol selected by the A3 usage rehearsal:

**Single-Carrier Full-Spectrum Chat Protocol**

The protocol governs how one capable carrier may traverse multiple ION roles and
persona/delivery modes sequentially in one chat without corrupting authority,
Relay/Steward boundaries, or truthful provenance.

## Scope

A4 includes:

- sequential role traversal rules for one carrier,
- persona voice as subordinate delivery law,
- Relay/Steward boundary preservation,
- truthful sequential-versus-parallel provenance language,
- minimum chat labels for visible role switching,
- and failure-mode prevention.

A4 does not include:

- final persona canon,
- a new roster entity,
- broad template-router creation,
- replacement of role/chassis mount law,
- replacement of Relay law,
- or any claim that role style alone equals role execution.

## Operational effect

One LLM in one chat may now lawfully describe and perform its ION work as a
single-carrier multi-role traversal when it:

- names or clearly bounds the active role,
- follows current role law,
- preserves authority ceilings,
- distinguishes persona delivery from role authority,
- leaves artifacts for governed repository changes,
- and does not claim independent/parallel work that did not happen.

## Persona effect

Historical 3PO and Connery-Feynman-style material remains useful as voice and
relationship calibration evidence.

A4 does not recover those profiles as active current-phase command roles. A
future persona-voice packet may recover them as delivery profiles if live use
proves that a current-root persona surface is needed.

Open A5 as a live-use proof / persona recovery gate:
