## Why the distinction matters

risks recreating the old pattern where a real center is discovered and then
immediately blurred again by oversized installation behavior.

Bounded thaw review is the correct bridge because it forces the trio to enter
active-architecture consideration through an explicit edit set and explicit
non-replacement boundaries.
