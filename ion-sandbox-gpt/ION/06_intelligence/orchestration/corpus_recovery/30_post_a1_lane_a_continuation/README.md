This folder records the bounded A2 continuation / reassessment packet after the
Lane A bridge pair landed on disk.

- `ION/02_architecture/META_TEMPLATE_CONSTITUTION_PROTOCOL.md`
- `ION/07_templates/actions/TEMPLATE_DEVELOPMENT.md`
- matching active current-phase registry records in
  `ION/03_registry/current_phase_template_surface_registry.yaml`

above the landed bridge pair.
