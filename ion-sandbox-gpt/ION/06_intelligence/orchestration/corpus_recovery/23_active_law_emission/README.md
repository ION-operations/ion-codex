# Active-law emission

This layer records the bounded moments where reviewed law is emitted into active
architecture without pretending that every supporting review artifact becomes
active law with it.

It exists to preserve:
- the exact emission packet,
- the active touch set actually used,
- the bounded adjacent clarifications completed during emission,
- and the narrow judgment that active-law emission has occurred while broader
  widening remains closed.

Current recorded emissions:
- the coupled activation/lifecycle emission pair,
- and the coupled runtime/session/API emission trio.
