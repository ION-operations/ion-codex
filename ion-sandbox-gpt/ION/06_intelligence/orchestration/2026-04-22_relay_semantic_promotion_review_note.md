## Current judgment

`Relay` now satisfies the current-phase semantic-promotion criteria strongly enough that promotion is recommended.

This should remain the **first** support-role semantic promotion in the current branch.

The reason is not prestige.
The reason is that Relay is the clearest persistent interface role in the live system, and the branch benefits immediately from making the front door more explicit.

## What promotion should and should not mean

Promotion should mean:

- stabilize the meaning of the role
- reduce front-door ambiguity
- preserve the distinction between `Steward`, `Codex`, and `Relay`
- keep Relay’s user-facing courier burden truthful across chassis changes

Promotion should **not** mean:

- give Relay architecture authority
- give Relay audit authority
- give Relay release authority
- let Relay silently become dispatch command

The lane and authority constraints in the existing protocol and boot should remain intact.

## Final judgment

`Relay` is no longer merely a useful live support role.
