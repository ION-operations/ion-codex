# T102 — Handoff capsule linkage and compact entry artifact

M15 must produce a compact entry artifact that preserves explicit links back to:
- the takeover-entry activation receipt,
- the continuation bundle root,
- the activation summary,
- and the role-session entry packet.

The capsule may be compact, but it must not sever lineage.
