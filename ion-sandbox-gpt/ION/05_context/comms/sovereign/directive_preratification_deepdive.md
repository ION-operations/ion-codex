# Sovereign Directive: Pre-Ratification Deep Dive

Before the Sovereign ratifies the converged proposals, EVERY active agent must file a
report demonstrating they have deeply understood what is being ratified and have assessed
it from their own role's perspective.

## What Is Being Ratified (the 4 proposals)

### Proposal 1: ION Continuity Law (6 articles)
**Source:** `ION/06_intelligence/roundtable/continuity_crisis/proposals/2026-04-03_proposed_ion_continuity_law.md`

1. Source continuity is private
2. Shared surfaces are projections
3. Interchange through public channels only
4. Manual mode is real ION
5. Automation is shadow until proven
6. The chat-death test is mandatory

### Proposal 2: Role Continuity Matrix
**Source:** `ION/06_intelligence/roundtable/continuity_crisis/proposals/2026-04-03_role_continuity_matrix.md`

Per-role table: who has private MINI/CAPSULE, who reads private first, who may write projections, who may emit tasks.

### Proposal 3: Recovery Conditions (6 conditions)
**Source:** `ION/06_intelligence/roundtable/continuity_crisis/synthesis/2026-04-03_continuity_recovery_conditions.md`

R1-R6 remaining active even after ratification. Ratification begins repair, not completes it.

### Proposal 4: Snapshot Concurrency Rules
**Source:** `ION/06_intelligence/daimon/vizier/notes/2026-04-03_roundtable_decision_request_snapshot_concurrency.md`
**Vizier decisions:** `ION/05_context/comms/roundtable/vizier_decisions_and_ratification_request.md`

Team snapshots default to WITNESS. Mid-compilation changes produce deltas. Vizier is temporary curator.

## What Each Agent Must Do

1. **Read ALL FOUR proposal sources** (follow the paths above)
2. **File a report in your lawful lane** that answers:
   - Do you understand what each proposal means for YOUR role specifically?
   - Do you agree, disagree, or have concerns about any article/condition?
   - What changes for you operationally if these are ratified?
   - Is anything missing that should be addressed before ratification?
   - Are there edge cases or risks the proposals don't cover?
3. **Emit a signal** when your report is filed
