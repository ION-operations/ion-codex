# V41_FRONT_STAGE_COUNCIL_RUNTIME_RECEIPTS Lock

Status: A3 runtime-inspection branch surface.  
Production authority: false.  
Persona total-ION identity: forbidden.  
Unreviewed high-impact user-facing claim emission: forbidden.

## Purpose

V41 converts the V40 Front-Stage Council doctrine into an executable receipt object.

The receipt records whether a proposed user-facing claim has:

1. Persona composition/rendering state;
2. Relay grounding/provenance state;
3. Steward/VZ claim-class and risk verdict;
4. user-facing emission permission;
5. required visibility level;
6. repair/retraction obligations if the claim cannot be emitted cleanly.

## Governance lock

Persona may remain fast, expressive, and conversational. However, any high-impact claim must have a council receipt before it is represented as verified ION state.

This lock does not require every backchannel or low-risk conversational utterance to block on full-system ratification. It introduces the runtime object needed to distinguish provisional speech from checked or ratified speech.
