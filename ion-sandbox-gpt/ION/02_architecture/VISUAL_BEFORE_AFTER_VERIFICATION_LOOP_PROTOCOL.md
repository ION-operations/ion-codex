# Visual Before/After Verification Loop Protocol

V48 compares before/after visual evidence at the level of observation packet references, diagnosis receipt references, evidence refs, local artifact hashes, and explicit finding transitions.

It prevents unsupported “looks fixed” claims by requiring a receipt state: verified, partial, regression/new issue, missing after evidence, change evidence requiring review, no actionable change, or blocked by Steward.

V48 does not grant browser control, production authority, persistent DOM mutation, credential-sensitive action, form submission, or destructive action.
