## Trigger

Phase 1 multi-agent rollout work raised a material question: should browser ChatGPT be
mounted directly into a live branch role now, or should it remain `EXTERNAL_UNMOUNTED`
and re-enter only through the browser zip-return bridge?

## Why Work Is Held

Direct browser role mount would widen authority, mount posture, and landing assumptions.
That widening cannot be treated as casual coordination preference because it changes how
external work re-enters the live branch.

## Required Artifact Set

- one Thoth `RESEARCH` artifact on current-phase evidence
- one Nemesis `AUDIT` artifact on protocol and contract conformance
- one Codex `PROPOSAL` artifact naming the bounded current-phase resolution
- one `HANDOFF` packet pointing at the next proof step
- one `SIGNAL` announcing the result

## Reconciliation Rule

Unless the research and audit surfaces show stronger current-phase mount law than the
existing browser default, browser ChatGPT remains `EXTERNAL_UNMOUNTED` and may only
participate through bounded external-return law.
