# Template Surface Change: Canonicalization Decision

## Purpose

Create one current-phase report template for adjudicating a competing artifact,
root, subsystem, or document cluster during controlled reintegration.

The active branch already has general proposal and research templates, but it
lacks one explicit surface for recording a canonicalization judgment with:

- competing candidates,
- chosen disposition,
- retained witnesses,
- confidence,
- and unresolved contradictions.

That gap is now material because the workspace has crossed from generic recovery
into controlled reintegration and root-adjudication work.

## Historical Search / Provenance

The recovery atlas and reintegration packets already encode many quasi-decision
surfaces:

- root-family judgments,
- center-status judgments,
- lane delta packets,
- review-entry packets,
- promotion-candidate packets.

But there is no small general-purpose template that says:

this candidate won, this candidate remains witness, this candidate is deferred,
and here is the evidence and confidence.

This template is therefore a current-phase extraction from real need, not a
claim that a historical constitutional decision template has been fully
restored.
