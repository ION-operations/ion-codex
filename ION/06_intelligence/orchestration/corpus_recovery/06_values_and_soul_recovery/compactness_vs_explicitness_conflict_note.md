# Compactness vs explicitness conflict note

One of the deepest recurring conflicts in the estate is:

- older compact executable lines preserve center visibility and low operator burden
- newer/current lines preserve more explicit governance repair and more visible law surfaces

The project must not silently assume:
- that compactness means incompleteness
- or that explicitness means better constitutional health

This conflict appears strongly in:
- production `ION` / `ION (codex branch)` vs current branch
- `IONv2` vs current branch
- older ION-BUILD meta-template center vs current branch bridge-template repair

This conflict is part of the soul/coherence audit, not a side note.
