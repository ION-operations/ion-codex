# Era 2 orchestration board

This layer exists to prevent controlled reintegration from degrading into passive accumulation.

Use it to answer, at any moment:

- what objective was just completed
- what lane is currently selected
- what the bounded horizon is
- what remains explicitly out of bounds
- what condition ends or redirects the current campaign

Current command surface:

- `current_era2_orchestration_board.md`
- `current_era2_orchestration_board_matrix.csv`
