# Pass 31 — Kernel governance outcomes note

## What this pass added

- canonical governance outcome classes
- governance outcome defaults
- governance review examples

## Why it matters

The kernel governance cadence already defined when review should happen and how health should be checked.
This pass adds the missing next step: what the review is allowed to conclude, in the smallest stable vocabulary possible.

That makes frozen-kernel governance more teachable and less likely to jump from “review” straight into oversized revision proposals.
