# Corpus recovery pass 4 enrichment note

This pass deepens Milestone 1 in three ways:

1. It upgrades the production precursor pair (`ION` and `ION (codex branch)`) from static profile witnesses to executable receipts.
2. It differentiates the broader SOS family into `SOS-OPUS` and `SOS-Gemini` as distinct supporting doctrine/consolidation witnesses.
3. It expands archive-variant handling with a machine-readable variant matrix and a production child-root matrix.

Why this matters:
- the atlas now treats compact runnable precursor lines as evidence, not memory
- the project can now tell more of the SOS family apart instead of flattening it
- archive duplication and witness variants are more explicit
