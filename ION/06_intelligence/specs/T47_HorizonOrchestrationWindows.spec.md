# T47 — Horizon Orchestration Windows

ION must maintain three lawful orchestration horizons:
- IMMEDIATE
- NEAR
- FAR

Each closer horizon requires stricter specificity and clearer executor/packet expectations.

Automation mode may change the carrier but must not change the horizon law.
