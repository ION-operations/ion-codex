# Canonicalization Decision: Retained Dual-Center Settlement

## Purpose

Resolve q005 honestly.

The current question is no longer whether the workspace contains a split-center
condition. That was already established. The question is whether the existing
evidence now authorizes a single-root ratification, or whether the truthful
current posture is a retained dual-center settlement.

## Scope

In scope:

1. current workspace root-authority settlement
2. whether single-root ratification is authorized now
3. how the packaged current-generation root and top-level production root
   should be treated after q001 through q004

Out of scope:

- execution of promotion packets
- code movement from top-level production `ION/`
- retirement of the top-level production root
- future reassessment after real promotion work lands

## Decision

### 1. Single-root ratification is not authorized now

Single-root workspace ratification is **not** authorized in the current phase.

The reason is straightforward:

- the production-only surfaces identified in q003 remain mapped, not promoted
- the top-level production root still carries real live extraction burden
- and the root-authority bundle in q004 solves startup drift without requiring
  a false claim of unification

### 2. Current truthful settlement

The current truthful workspace settlement is a
**retained dual-center settlement**.

That means:

- the packaged current-generation root is the primary operational and
  reintegration center
- the top-level production root is retained as a secondary extraction and
  promotion center
- the embedded residue lane remains non-root witness matter under the packaged
  center

### 3. This is a settlement, not only a deferral

This packet is not just another "not yet decided" placeholder.

It establishes the active law for the present phase:

- do not reopen the root-authority question ad hoc for routine work
- do not describe the workspace as already single-root unified
- do not treat the top-level production root as retired
- do treat the bundle as the current stable onboarding surface for this
  settlement

### 4. Operating rule after settlement

Routine rule:

- start in the packaged current-generation root

Escalation rule:

- enter top-level production `ION/` only when the task is explicitly about
  the q003 production-only surface classes or their future promotion packets

### 5. Bundle status after settlement

The q004 bundle is now the first stable carrier-facing startup surface for the
retained dual-center settlement.

It remains transitional in the long horizon, but it is no longer merely a
working placeholder for the current phase.

### 6. Reassessment rule

Single-root ratification may be reopened only after bounded promotion packets
land real changes in the packaged root, at minimum across the Class A floor and
whatever additional q003 classes are required to eliminate the top-level
production root's live extraction burden honestly.

Until then, retained dual-center settlement is the governing answer.

## Retained witnesses and deferred matter

Retained live centers:

- packaged current-generation root
- top-level production root

Retained witness matter:

- embedded residue lane inside the packaged root
- AIM-ION as adjacent sibling witness pack / selective pattern reservoir

Deferred matter:

- actual promotion execution packets
- any later retirement or tombstoning of top-level production `ION/`
- any future reopening of single-root ratification

## Confidence and unresolved contradictions

Confidence: **high** that single-root ratification is not supportable yet.

Confidence: **high** that retained dual-center settlement is the truthful
current answer.

Confidence: **medium** on how many future promotion packets will be necessary
before honest single-root reassessment is possible.

Unresolved contradictions that must remain visible:

- the packaged root remains the stronger reintegration center, but still lacks
  production-side surfaces mapped in q003
- the top-level production root remains live enough that calling it mere stale
  archive would be false
- the bundle stabilizes startup truth, but it does not by itself collapse two
  centers into one

## Required follow-up

1. Update the reintegration registries from provisional partition to retained
   dual-center settlement.
2. Update the startup bundle and entry surfaces so they state settlement
   truthfully.
3. Treat future single-root discussion as a new bounded reassessment packet,
   not as an ambient assumption.
