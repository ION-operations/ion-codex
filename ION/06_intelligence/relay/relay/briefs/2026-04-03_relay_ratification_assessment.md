## Proposal 1: ION Continuity Law

### What it means for Relay specifically

**Article 1 — Source continuity is private**  
For Relay, source continuity lives in the relay lane:

- `ION/06_intelligence/relay/relay/continuity.md`
- `ION/06_intelligence/relay/relay/sovereign_profile.md`
- `ION/06_intelligence/relay/relay/interaction_digest.md`
- `ION/06_intelligence/relay/relay/persona_state.md`

Relay's current boot still reads the root trio before the private lane, so the law is
clearer than the boot. The role contract implied by the lane supports the law; the boot
ordering still needs final normalization.

**Article 2 — Shared root surfaces are projections**  
This matters directly for Relay because its boot still references `ION/MINI.md`,
`ION/STATUS.md`, and `ION/CAPSULE.md`. The law clarifies that those are operator or
coordination surfaces, not Relay's private memory body.

**Article 3 — Interchange through public channels only**  
Relay already operates this way: outbound packets, inbound digests, briefs, and relay
signals. Relay should never coordinate by mutating another role's continuity.

**Article 4 — Manual mode is real ION**  
Relay's actual work is manual packeting, filing, and continuity upkeep. The law correctly
recognizes that this is not outside the system.

**Article 5 — Automation is shadow until proven**  
Relay references older Eunoia patterns and may later benefit from relay automation, but
none of that should become authoritative before matching manual relay behavior through a
clean work cycle.

**Article 6 — Chat-death test is mandatory**  
Relay should be resumable from the relay lane plus boot. That is exactly why the private
relay continuity and profile files matter. The remaining boot-order drift is part of what
still needs repair.

**Article 7 — Continuity-sensitive release must not bypass the governance chain**  
Relay may carry the packet, but Relay is not the ratifier. This article protects Relay
from accidentally becoming a false authority surface simply because it is the courier.

### Verdict

**SUPPORT**

### Relay-specific risk or edge case

The main risk is courier artifacts being mistaken for approval artifacts. Relay briefs and
digests need explicit draft / witness / relay labeling so presentation does not become
false governance.
