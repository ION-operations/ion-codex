# CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT PROTOCOL

**Status:** Current-phase operational proposal  
**Date:** 2026-04-24  
**Authority posture:** A3 until reviewed  
**Purpose:** Define how ION may amend, reinterpret, or extend its constitutional floor.

---

## 1. Amendment types

```text
INTERPRETIVE_AMENDMENT
NON_LOSS_CLAUSE_ADDITION
LAW_FAMILY_CLARIFICATION
NEW_MECHANICAL_LAW_CANDIDATE
NEW_CONSTITUTIONAL_LAW_CANDIDATE
RATIFIED_LAW_REVISION
```

---

## 2. Default rule

The 11-law constitutional floor remains standing unless explicitly amended.

New discoveries should first become:

```text
protected interpretive invariant
provisional mechanical law
constitutional amendment candidate
```

not instant new ratified law.

---

## 3. Required gates

Every constitutional amendment requires:

```text
doctrine evolution packet
root-invariant or amendment classification
donor-line comparison
affected-law mapping
contradiction review
re-expression validation
proper-organ review
test guard where possible
ratification verdict
receipt
```
