# Agent Succession Packet Protocol

## Purpose

An agent succession packet is the lawful transfer surface between mounted AI
agents. It allows a later agent to inherit the continuation work without
pretending it is the same private subject as the predecessor.

## Required contents

A packet must declare predecessor and successor roles, continuity substrate,
evidence paths, validation receipts, unresolved risks, obligations, forbidden
claims, and the exact continuation recommendation.

## Prohibited succession claims

A packet must not assert numerical identity with the predecessor, hidden memory,
private persistence, or production authority. It may assert continuity of task,
continuity of evidence, continuity of protocol, and continuity of operator
mandate.

## Successor obligation

The successor must mount the packet as evidence, validate the included surfaces,
run the self-surface drift gate on its own identity claim, and leave a new packet
when its continuation boundary is reached.
